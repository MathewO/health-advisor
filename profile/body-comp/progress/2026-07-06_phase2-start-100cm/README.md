## Rex assessment (6 Jul 2026)

**Overall:** Phase 2 starts from a well-maintained position. Maintenance delivered exactly what it was designed to do — minimal fat regain, full hormonal/metabolic recovery window, and a scale that settled close to Phase 1 end. The visual gap vs the April baseline is clearly banked. The lower belly and love handles are the defined targets for the next 8 weeks.

**Front (01):** Fuller midsection than the depleted Jun 4 state (+1.4 kg, mostly glycogen) — expected and not concerning. The waist-to-shoulder taper established in Phase 1 is intact. The ribcage-to-hip narrowing that wasn't visible at the April baseline is still present. Upper chest and pectoral definition visible against the reduced midsection. Lower belly above the waistband is the primary Phase 2 target.

**Left side — sleeve (02):** The back/shoulder sleeve tattoo makes this a distinctive and striking set. Belly projection in relaxed standing is slightly more than the depleted Jun 4 state — consistent with glycogen fill — but sits clearly behind the chest line. This structural improvement from Phase 1 has been preserved through maintenance. Posterior chain (upper back, lat sweep) looks broad and well-developed, confirming muscle retention.

**Right side — mushroom (03):** Consistent with (02). Natural relaxed projection in this pose is meaningfully less than the April baseline. The glycogen rebound accounts for the slight increase from Phase 1 end; the underlying composition improvement is still visible. Remaining soft tissue above the waistband is the Phase 2 target.

**Side relaxed (04):** Good lateral waist reference. The oblique line from armpit to hip is visible — the love handle that was clearly present at the April baseline has been meaningfully reduced. The torso silhouette from this angle is markedly different to the early-phase sets. Soft tissue on the lateral waist is the Phase 2 secondary target.

**Arms raised (05):** The honest diagnostic. Lower belly soft tissue present under the arm-raise stretch — fully expected at ~27% BF with full glycogen fill. The projection is slightly more than the depleted Jun 4 state but materially less than the April baseline. Love handle from this angle is reduced. This pose will be the clearest visual indicator of Phase 2 progress over 8 weeks.

**Key observation:** Waist at 100 cm is exactly where it was mid-maintenance (14 Jun). The maintenance month produced ≤1 cm waist change from Phase 1 end, confirming the rebound is glycogen/water only. Comparing these photos directly to the Jun 4 set gives a clean visual confirmation that trunk fat hasn't meaningfully increased.

**Phase 2 target context:**
- At 75 kg (target), Navy formula projects **~23–24% BF** (waist ~95 cm, assuming ~1 cm per kg loss)
- That represents ~5 kg fat loss from today, reaching Layer 2→3 boundary — visible lower belly/love handle reduction, approaching the Bali goal
- Phase 2 projected end (75 kg): fat mass ~17 kg (vs today's ~22 kg) = **−5 kg fat**, assuming 85–90% of weight loss is fat
