## Rex assessment (4 Jun 2026)

**Overall:** Phase 1 delivered in full. −6.0 kg and −9 cm waist in 50 days from baseline, against a target of −3.8 kg (to 82 kg). This is the clearest set of the phase for visible composition change.

**Front (01):** The torso shape has genuinely changed. The waist-to-shoulder taper is now visible from the front — the ribcage-to-hip narrowing that wasn't there at the Apr 15 baseline when the midsection was essentially cylindrical. The lower belly sits notably flatter; the belly button area no longer projects forward. Upper chest and pectoral development is now clearly visible with the midsection reduction providing contrast. The remaining soft tissue is concentrated in the lower belly just above the waistband — classic android distribution, exactly where you'd expect it to be last.

**Left side (02):** The single most diagnostic pose for android trunk fat. The belly profile now sits clearly behind the chest line in full relaxation — a meaningful structural change versus April where the belly projection matched or exceeded the chest. The chest is definitively forward of the midsection. Lower belly soft tissue remains but is substantially reduced in both volume and projection. The posterior chain (upper back, lat sweep through the dragon tattoo) looks broad and well-developed — the muscle base hasn't moved, which is exactly what you want after a 6-week deficit.

**Right side (03):** Consistent with the left side. The belly profile in natural relaxed standing is considerably reduced from the May 28 set and dramatically different from the April baseline. The mushroom arm tattoo is visible; the dog remains reliable in his position. The remaining soft tissue above the waistband is the primary remaining target for Phase 2.

**Right side relaxed (04):** Lateral waist contour has improved — the love handle that was clearly present at the Apr 15 and Apr 29 baselines is substantially reduced. The oblique line from armpit to hip is emerging. Upper back width and the lat sweep are prominent in this angle, reinforcing good muscle retention. The torso silhouette from this angle is noticeably different to the early-phase sets.

**Arms raised / abdominal stretch (05):** Lower belly skin still present under the stretch — fully expected at ~23% BF. The projection under the stretch is materially less than the May 28 arms-raised shot; the belly doesn't dome forward as it did. Love handle from this angle is soft and reduced compared to all prior sets. This pose is the honest one — it shows what's left to work on in Phase 2.

**Key observation vs May 28:** Another −1 cm waist in 7 days (100 → 99 cm) alongside −0.8 kg. The rate of visible change has been consistent and accelerating through Layer 2 of the belly fat model. The lower belly and love handle are the remaining primary targets — exactly as predicted by the layer model when planning Phase 2.

**Phase 1 verdict:** Target was 82 kg / ~102–104 cm waist. Delivered: 79.0 kg / 99 cm waist, 3 kg and 3–5 cm ahead of target with full strength preservation (squat 100 kg, deadlift 140 kg, DB press new rep PB). Visual progress clearly visible across all five poses. Phase 2 (post-Italy, ~75–77 kg / ~19–21% BF) has an excellent starting position.
