## Rex assessment (22 Jul 2026)

**Overall:** 16 days into Phase 2, the first meaningful visual changes from the cut are beginning to show. At −2.5 kg from phase start and −1.1 kg below the Phase 1 depleted low, this is the leanest set to date. The composition change is real but still in the early stages — the most visible progress will come from week 3 onward as the lower belly and love handle depots start moving more meaningfully.

**Front (01):** The waist-to-shoulder taper established in Phase 1 is intact and slightly more pronounced than the Jul 6 start set. The lower belly above the waistband is the clear remaining target. Upper chest and pec definition visible. Compared to Phase 1 end (79.0 kg depleted), this set at 77.9 kg with glycogen on board is the better structural position — leaner absolute weight, just with more water.

**Left side — sleeve (02):** The belly projection in relaxed standing is marginally less than the Jul 6 set (80.4 kg). The chest sits clearly forward of the midsection. The large back/shoulder sleeve tattoo makes this the most distinctive pose for visual change tracking. Posterior chain (upper back, shoulder) looks well-developed — muscle retention confirmed.

**Right side — mushroom (03):** Consistent with (02). The natural relaxed belly profile is slightly reduced from Jul 6. The lower belly soft tissue above the waistband remains the Phase 2 primary target.

**Side relaxed (04):** The oblique line from armpit to hip is visible. Love handle is present but clearly less than the Apr 15 baseline. Good lateral waist comparison angle — improvements here will be a key visual indicator in the coming weeks.

**Arms raised (05):** The honest diagnostic. Lower belly under the arm-raise stretch shows soft tissue — expected at this body composition and glycogen level. The overall projection is noticeably less than the Phase 2 start set. This pose will show the most meaningful change as Phase 2 progresses.

**Key comparison vs Jul 6 (phase start, 80.4 kg):** 2.5 kg lighter, visually slightly leaner across all angles. The glycogen is still on board (same conditions), so this is a clean direct comparison. The changes are real — not dramatic at 16 days, but the trajectory is exactly right.

**Key comparison vs Jun 4 (Phase 1 end, 79.0 kg depleted):** At 77.9 kg refed today vs 79.0 kg depleted then, the absolute weight is lower by 1.1 kg. The slight visual similarity in belly profile between the two sets is due to glycogen fill — the actual fat mass is lower today than it was on Jun 4.

**What to expect next:** By weeks 4–6, if the phase continues at current rate (~0.65–0.75 kg/wk real fat loss), the lower belly profile will show meaningful visible reduction in all side poses. The love handle in pose 04 should be noticeably reduced by the next photo set. Target for next photos: ~2–3 weeks (around Aug 5–12).
