## Rex assessment (13 May 2026)

**Overall:** Phase is clearly working. 4 weeks in, −4.2 kg and −5 cm waist. All fat loss is from the central/trunk depot as expected with classic android distribution.

**Front (01):** Lower belly and waist still the dominant feature, but compared to baseline (~28.5% BF) the reduction is visible — the overhang is less pronounced and the side flanks have narrowed. Upper chest and shoulders show solid muscle development.

**Left side (02):** Belly profile still prominent but the anterior projection has reduced. The lower belly soft tissue above the waistband is still the main remaining feature — this is the last-to-go Layer 2 zone per the belly fat projections.

**Right side (03):** Consistent with left side. The belly is still clearly present in profile but at 80 kg (~23.6% BF) this should reduce meaningfully further.

**Rear views (04–05):** Love handles visible from behind but the back and shoulder musculature (lats, traps, rear delts) is well-developed and becoming more visible as the overlying fat reduces. No muscle wasting signal — upper body looks solid.

**Key observation:** The chest and upper body look proportionally leaner than the midsection — consistent with android fat distribution where the trunk is the last to clear. Arms and back are ahead of the belly in the reveal.

**Prediction for 80 kg set (~early June):** ~100–101 cm waist. Lower belly overhang should be noticeably reduced. Love handles from behind should be softer. Upper body definition will be more apparent.
