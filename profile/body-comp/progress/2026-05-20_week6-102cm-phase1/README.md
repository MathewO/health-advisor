## Rex assessment (20 May 2026)

**Overall:** Visible, measurable progress in 35 days. −5.1 kg and −6 cm waist. Even in the one week since the May 13 set, the lower belly profile has softened noticeably. Right on track for the 80 kg / 100–101 cm phase-end target.

**Front (01):** The waist narrowing is clear vs the May 13 set — the flank taper from ribcage to hip is more defined. Lower belly overhang is still present (expected at ~24% BF — this is Layer 2 territory) but less dominant than at baseline. Upper chest and shoulder width look solid — no visible muscle loss.

**Left side (02):** The anterior belly projection is measurably less pronounced than the May 13 shot. The lower soft tissue above the waistband has reduced. Back musculature — lats and rear delt — is visible and well-preserved. Love handle bulk from this angle is diminishing but still present, consistent with android fat distribution losing from the top down.

**Right side (03):** Consistent with left side. Belly profile in the relaxed position is visibly softer than May 13. The chest is now forward of the lower belly in profile, which wasn't as clear at baseline — a meaningful visual milestone.

**Rear right (04):** Upper back musculature clearly visible — traps and rear delts developed. Love handles from behind have reduced. Waist-to-hip differential more apparent than in earlier sets.

**Arms raised / abdominal stretch (05):** Lower belly skin and fat still present under stretch — expected at this BF%. The love handle from behind is softer than the May 13 arms-raised shot. Core is being revealed from the top; the lower section will be the last to go.

**Key observation vs May 13:** The most notable change is the belly profile in the side shots — the anterior projection has reduced more than the waist measurement alone (−1 cm) would suggest. This may reflect continued reduction in visceral fat (which doesn't show in tape measurements) alongside the subcutaneous layer. Upper body definition is ahead of the midsection, consistent with the android pattern.

**Prediction for phase-end set (~Jun 6, ~79–80 kg):** ~100–101 cm waist. Lower belly overhang further reduced but likely still present. Love handles from behind noticeably softer. Upper body definition markedly more visible. This is not the endpoint for the midsection — Phase 2 (post-Italy, targeting ~75–77 kg) is where that completes.
