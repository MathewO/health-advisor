## Rex assessment (28 May 2026)

**Overall:** The most significant visual change of the phase. −6 kg and −8 cm waist in 45 days. The 100 cm milestone hit on the same day as this set — the phase-end prediction has been met with 9 days to spare.

**Front (01):** The waist taper is now clearly visible from the front — the narrowing from ribcage to hip is distinct compared to the Apr 15 baseline where the torso was more cylindrical. The lower belly overhang has reduced significantly; the belly button area now sits noticeably flatter. Upper chest and shoulder width look proportionally larger as the midsection has reduced — a hallmark of android fat distribution being cleared from the trunk. Chest definition is emerging.

**Left side (02):** The most striking change versus baseline. The anterior belly projection — which was prominent and forward of the chest line in April — is now substantially reduced. The chest is clearly forward of the belly in profile, a milestone that wasn't there at the start. Lower belly soft tissue above the waistband remains but is visibly smaller. The upper back and shoulder musculature is well-defined through the dragon tattoo.

**Right side (03):** Consistent with the left side. The belly profile in full relaxation is closer to the chest line than at any previous set. The dog is still in position. The lower belly is the remaining primary soft tissue feature — consistent with android Layer 2 pattern.

**Rear right (04):** Love handles visibly reduced compared to May 20 and especially baseline. Upper back width and lat development are increasingly apparent. Waist-to-hip ratio has improved materially.

**Arms raised / abdominal stretch (05):** Lower belly skin still present under stretch — expected at ~23.5% BF. The love handle from the side is noticeably softer than in earlier arms-raised shots. The stretch reveals how much visceral reduction has occurred — the belly doesn't project forward as dramatically as it did at baseline.

**Key observation vs May 20:** The 8-day period from May 20 to May 28 produced the most visible change per week of the phase. This is consistent with the belly fat layer model — you crossed deeper into Layer 2 (~24%→~23.5% BF) where subcutaneous and visceral fat reduce more rapidly. The acceleration is real, not just a perception effect.

**Phase-end prediction (Jun 6, ~79 kg 7d avg):** ~99–100 cm waist. The lower belly will be softer still. This set already shows the phase largely delivering on its visual promise. Phase 2 (post-Italy, targeting ~75–77 kg / ~19–21% BF) is where the remaining lower belly and love handles get addressed in earnest.
