## Interpretation Guide

- **Losing > 0.75 kg/week**: deficit too aggressive — muscle loss risk. Increase calories by ~100–150 kcal.
- **Losing 0.3–0.75 kg/week**: on track — maintain current approach.
- **Losing < 0.25 kg/week for 2+ weeks**: deficit too small — reduce calories by ~100 kcal or add light cardio.
- **Weight up week-on-week**: don't panic — check if training, sleep, or sodium/water could explain it. Look at 2-week trend.
