## Method Notes

- Brown beef and turkey mince together in a pan
- Add diced onion, mushrooms, passata — simmer to make bolognese sauce
- Make béchamel with butter, milk, and parmesan
- Layer: pasta sheets → bolognese → béchamel, repeat, finish with béchamel and grated parmesan
- Bake ~40 min at 180°C until golden
